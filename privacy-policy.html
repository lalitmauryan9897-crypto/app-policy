Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Fluntagrameh respects user privacy. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile app.

Information We Collect

We collect basic information necessary to provide app functionality and enhance your experience:

  • Username - For account identification
  • Email Address - For authentication and notifications
  • Profile Information - Bio, profile picture, and preferences
  • Uploaded Content - Media files and posts you share
  • Usage Data - App interactions and analytics (anonymized)
Minimal Collection User-Controlled Privacy-First

How We Use Information

Your information is used solely to enhance your experience:

  • Authentication - Secure login and account management
  • App Features - Delivering core app functionality
  • User Experience - Improving app performance and features
  • Communication - Sending important updates and notifications (with your consent)
  • Analytics - Understanding usage patterns (anonymized data only)

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption - Data transmitted using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Secure Storage - Information stored in secure, protected databases
  • Access Control - Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits - Continuous monitoring and security updates
  • Compliance - Adherence to international data protection standards

Third-Party Services

Our Data Policy

We do not sell user data to third parties. Your personal information is never sold or shared for commercial purposes.

Essential Integrations

We may use third-party services only for essential functionality:

  • Cloud storage providers (encrypted and secure)
  • Analytics tools (anonymized data only)
  • Payment processors (PCI-DSS compliant)

These partners are bound by strict data protection agreements and privacy commitments.

Children's Privacy

Fluntagrameh is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.
